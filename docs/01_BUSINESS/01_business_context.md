# Business Context
This document provides a concise overview of the business context, goals, stakeholders, scope.

## Purpose
Provide a web-based flashcard platform using spaced repetition (SRS) for efficient learning.

## Business Goals
- Enable users to manage flashcard sets.
- Support SRS-based learning and progress tracking.
- Allow browsing and copying of public sets.
- Support language flashcards with hints.

## Scope
### In Scope
- User account management.
- Flashcard set creation, editing, deletion, and copying.
- Study sessions with SRS and progress tracking.
- Browsing public sets.

### Out of Scope
- Offline functionality.
- Collaborative editing.
